About me

Hi, my name is Ana Aleksova. I am a 19 years old and I live in Skopje, North Macedonia. I have finished my high school education at Orce Nikolov and now I am a student at the Faculty of Computer Science & Engineering. As a beginner in the IT field, I am driven by curiosity and a passion to make a difference. I enjoy exploring various aspects of technology on my own, embracing challenges and expanding my knowledge. I am a very hardworking and creative person who can neatly fit in a group and can easily assume the role of the leader. Additionally, I am keen on traveling and it is my personal goal to travel around the whole world, visit exotic places and learn about new cultures.
